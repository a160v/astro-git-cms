Welcome! This site is powered by **astro-git-cms** — a tiny, git-based CMS.

Every post, note and picture here is a Markdown file in a git repository.
Publishing means committing. There is no database, no server, and nothing to
maintain: the site is rebuilt and redeployed automatically on every push.

## Writing from anywhere

Open `/admin` on this site (yes, even on a phone) and you can:

- write posts, notes and pages
- upload pictures
- save drafts
- cross-post to the fediverse and Bluesky
- send a post as a newsletter

All of it goes through the Forgejo API, so your content never leaves your own
repository.
